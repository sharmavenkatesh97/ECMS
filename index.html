Why Choose This Portal?

We are better than a lot of already existing portals and differ from them in a alot of ways!!

Unbiased Automisation
The machine learning model that we have used in this ECMS portal makes the entire process unbiased and completely driven by the automatic system that enatils the details of the filed complaint and predicts the number of days in which the complaints will either be solved or escalated to another level for future references and solutions, thereby making our product unique from the other portals that are available.
Transparency
You can always keep a check on the grievances you have filed, the number of days that the problem will take to be solved or escalated and the number of times the previous grievences that had been filed without any human intervention and any biased ruling of the same. the complaints against the highest authority can also be filed and a continuous check can also be kept on the same.
On-field personal
We have a provision for the on-field personal where he can go and visit the site and also have a one on one view of the site and give his perception thereby keeping the sole purpose of filing a complain and reachimg out to the authorities safe-guarded.
